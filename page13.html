About us

Jacq in a box was started as a business in 2014 selling giftware we have progressed to selling clothes, jewellery, handbags.

In 2015 the Poppies Plants and Flowers florist business next shop to us was put up for sale we bought it and now include flowers.


Jacqueline who owns and runs the business is a florist. We have incorporated it into Jacq in a Box. We invite you to come along and have a look at the giftware and the flowers. We think you will be delighted at the variety and quality of the products that we sell.

We are now in the Colonnades Shopping Centre at Noarlunga
having closed our Aldinga shop.

Our network of local florists will hand arrange whichever flowers you choose and can deliver your gift Australia wide as well as global.

We also welcome phone orders for flowers.
