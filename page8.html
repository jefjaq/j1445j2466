Our Flowers

 When words aren’t enough, say thank you with flowers, plants and gifts. Show your appreciation and gratitude for all that they have done with a bouquet bursting with warm and colourful flowers, or an artistic arrangement that will have them smiling from ear to ear! Popular thank you flowers include sweet smelling lilies and cheerful daisies, while our range of plants are a great idea for a more long lasting gift.
Whether you’d like to say thanks for a job well done, for looking after your pet while you were away or for helping you organise a special event, Petals’ range of thank you flowers will bring joy and happiness to the lucky recipient.
