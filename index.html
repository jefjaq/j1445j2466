Our Flowers

 Flowers are kept in a cool room and the ones that are on display are put back in there every night, this keeps the flowers fresh and gives them a much longer life. Flower food is given with all flower boxes, bouquets and arrangements sold.
With water changes and flower food they could last for up to three weeks. 

Our network of local florists will hand arrange whichever Flowers you choose and can deliver your gift Australia wide.
